Overview

Frostbites, a Canadian company known for its freeze-dried candies and fruits, recognized that their current branding and packaging were no longer resonating with their customers. They felt that the design was outdated and failed to capture the vibrant and unique essence of their products.

To address this, the client sought a complete redesign to infuse their brand with new energy and personality. This included updating the visual identity of their freeze-dried fruit line and introducing a new kiwi product. The project involved creating a fresh logo and designing pouch packaging for four different fruits, aiming to enhance shelf appeal and better reflect the quality of their offerings.

Process

Through extensive research and competitive analysis I determined how other similar products are positioning themselves as a brand. Using that data and client requests I was able to come up with 4-5 design versions of one fruit. Upon client review and approval of their favorite design, I designed the rest of the fruits and mocked them up to present the final packaging product.
