Who Am I ?

I’m Sami, a 21 year old Graphic and Web Designer based out of Vancouver, BC. I’m currently studying New Media Design and Web Development at BCIT with a goal of pursuing a career in the design field.

I love to create brand identities, packaging, websites and motion graphics! I grew up loving to create art in all different forms, but ever since being passed down an old laptop I absolutely fell in love with digital art and design.

When im away from my screens, you can find me at the gym working out or playing soccer, playing with my cat Bruno or preparing a nice delicious meal!
